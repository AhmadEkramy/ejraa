Specialized Clinic License Conditions and Papers (Polyclinic)

Definition

A medical complex that includes more than one specialty, with a minimum of two specialties, and no maximum limit depending on the number of rooms and available space.

Room Distribution

  • For every two specialties: one equipped room, except for the following specialties:
  • Dentistry, Gynecology, and Ophthalmology: separate room for each due to special equipment
  • Other specialties: can combine two specialties in one room

Authorized Persons for Licensing

  • Facility Owner: General practitioner, specialist, or consultant
  • Technical Manager: Specialist or consultant only (general practitioners not allowed)
  • Name approval required for clinics (trade name) - no duplicate names in the same governorate
  • This step is mandatory before making the sign
  • Only the owner's and technical manager's names appear on the license

Adding Other Specialties

  • Done through contracts registered with the syndicate
  • Other doctors don't need to be present or have power of attorney, but must be informed
  • Their names are not mentioned in the license or syndicate registration

Location Requirements

  • Number of rooms matches number of specialties, each room with desk + examination bed + screen + specialty tools
  • Some specialties require a sink
  • Special equipment for certain specialties (dental equipment - gynecology equipment - ophthalmology equipment)
