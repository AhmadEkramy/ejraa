Private Clinic License

Complete licensing solution for individual medical practitioners

Starting from 12,000 - 16,000 EGP

Private Clinic License Conditions and Papers

License Type

Private clinic license in the doctor's name (Private Clinic)

General Conditions

  • Personal license in the doctor's name
  • Sign with doctor's name only
  • All documents in the doctor's name
  • Trade name not allowed
  • Any human or dental specialty allowed to open a private clinic

Cost

  • Human specialties: 12,000 EGP including fees, expenses, and official fees for all procedures until license receipt
  • Dental: 12,000 to 16,000 EGP including contracts and expenses, varies according to number of dental units

Clinic Location Requirements

  • Complete apartment or room within an apartment

Basic Required Documents

  • National ID card
  • Ownership or rental contract
  • Specialist or consultant certificate
  • Professional practice license (for general practitioners, attach copy of graduation certificate)
  • Power of attorney directed to the company to proceed with procedures

Additional Documents According to Doctor's Status

  • If employed: employment status statement
  • If not employed: free doctor certificate
  • Original professional practice license
  • Copy of university certificate
  • Latest qualification obtained by the doctor
